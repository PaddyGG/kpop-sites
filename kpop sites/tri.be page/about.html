ABOUT

TRI.BE (트라이비), formerly known as LIONGIRLS (라이언걸즈) and TRI.ANGLE (트라이앵글), is a 7 member girl group under TR Entertainment and Mellow Entertainment in Korea and Republic Records in the US, formerly under Lionheart Entertainment and are co-managed by Universal Music. They debuted on February 17th, 2021 with the single album “Da Loca”.

Name Explanation: The group’s name is a combination of “TRI”, an abbreviation for “triangle”, which is a symbol of perfection, and “BE”, which means existence, which has the meaning of ‘perfect existence’.
